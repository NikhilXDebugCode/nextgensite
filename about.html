Our Story

A Startup on a Mission

NextGen Forge Technologies was founded in 2024 with a bold mission: to make enterprise-grade technology accessible to every business, regardless of size. From day one, we set out to combine startup agility with world-class engineering.

In just over 2 years, we have delivered more than 120 projects spanning AI, software engineering, cybersecurity, and cloud infrastructure. Our focus has always remained the same — real results, real value, real fast.

Today, headquartered at our Pune head office in Baner, we are a rapidly growing team trusted by businesses across industries to help them build, scale, and secure their digital future.
