Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 13, 2026 — We are committed to protecting your privacy and personal data.

This Privacy Policy describes how NextGen Forge Technologies ("we," "us," or "our"), headquartered at Baner, Pune, Maharashtra, India, collects, uses, and protects the personal information you provide when using our website or services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information: Personal Information (name, email address, phone number, resume) provided voluntarily through contact or job application forms; Usage Data (IP address, browser type, pages visited, time on site) collected automatically when you visit our website; and Cookie Data as described in our Cookie Policy.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to: respond to your inquiries and service requests; process and evaluate job applications; send newsletters or updates (only with your explicit consent); improve our website and services; comply with legal obligations; and prevent fraudulent or illegal activities.

3. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. We may share data with trusted third-party service providers (such as email delivery services and analytics platforms) strictly to operate our website and services, subject to confidentiality agreements. We may disclose information when required by law or legal process.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures aligned with ISO 27001 standards to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to: access the personal data we hold about you; request correction of inaccurate data; request deletion of your data (subject to legal obligations); withdraw consent at any time; and lodge a complaint with a relevant data protection authority. To exercise these rights, contact us at hr@nextgenforgetechnologies.com.

6. Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience, analyze traffic, and remember your preferences. You may control cookie settings through your browser or via the cookie consent banner on our website. Declining optional cookies will not affect core site functionality.

7. Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, or as required by applicable law. Job application data is retained for a maximum of 12 months after the recruitment process concludes.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of our website after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
